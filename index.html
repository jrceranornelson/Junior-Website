About Me

"I believe that transforming a nation requires more than politics — it requires education, institutions, ethics, and people who dare to build something that lasts."

Junior Ceranor Nelson is a Haitian entrepreneur, diplomat, and international development professional dedicated to advancing diplomacy, education, and humanitarian progress. As Founder of UNHDIP, Doredu, Omnedu, and Bòkatedral, International Cooperation Officer at Quisqueya University, and Co-founder of Haiti Stole My Heart Foundation, he leads initiatives that promote ethical leadership, expand opportunity, and strengthen international collaboration. Anchored in a bold vision for Haiti's future, he works to build lasting impact through innovation, service, and strategic partnership.

Diplomacy & Development

UNHDIP

UNHDIP, short for United Honorary Diplomats, is a global platform dedicated to connecting, empowering, and elevating honorary diplomats through international cooperation, dialogue, and strategic engagement. It works to strengthen diplomatic relationships, promote shared initiatives, and highlight the important role honorary diplomats play in building bridges between nations, institutions, and communities.

Education

OMNEDU

OMNEDU is an innovative educational platform committed to making quality learning more accessible, practical, and inclusive. Guided by the motto Instructio Pro Omnibus ("Education for All"), it aims to empower individuals and communities through affordable, relevant, and transformative education.

Education & Social Impact

DOREDU

DOREDU is a trademark registered nonprofit platform and organization in the USA, focused on expanding access to empower underserved communities through affordable, culturally relevant education that improves skills, integration, and opportunity. It helps immigrants, refugees, and marginalized populations gain useful skills for work and daily life.
